Our Story

Connecting breeders in Butuan City through technology, trust, and a shared passion for animal welfare.

Why We Started

BreedLink was born from a simple observation: people in Butuan City struggled to find trusted animal breeding partners with verified health documents — making healthy breeding difficult and risky.

As students, we created this platform to solve that problem. We envisioned a secure digital hub where every animal's health history, vaccination records, and genetic lineage could be safely stored and easily accessed by verified breeders and owners.

We're proud to help breeders — from small-scale farmers to professional kennel operators — connect with confidence, ensuring healthier offspring through transparent, document‑secured matches.
